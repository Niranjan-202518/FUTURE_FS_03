Our Story

A Sanctuary of Sound & Soul

Ragaalayam was founded with a single, heartfelt mission — to make the beauty of music accessible to every soul in Bangalore. Nestled in the heart of Rajajinagar, our academy blends the richness of Indian classical traditions with the excitement of contemporary music.

Whether you're a curious beginner picking up an instrument for the first time, or an advanced learner deepening your mastery of a raga, Ragaalayam is your home.
