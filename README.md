## ✨ Features

- **Responsive Design** — Fully mobile-friendly with hamburger menu
- **Hero Section** — Animated floating music notes, instrument cards, stat counters
- **About Section** — Academy story, highlights, and tag badges
- **Courses Section** — 6 detailed course cards (Carnatic, Hindustani, Percussion, Guitar, Keyboard, Drums)
- **Why Us Section** — 6 key differentiators with icons
- **Contact & Enrollment Form** — Free trial class booking form with success feedback
- **Contact Details** — Phone, email, location, and timings
- **Scroll Reveal Animations** — Elements fade in as you scroll
- **Animated Stat Counters** — Numbers count up on scroll into view
- **Active Nav Highlighting** — Nav link highlights based on current section
- **SEO Optimized** — Meta title, description, and keywords for local search

## 🎯 Business Pitch Summary

**Problem:** Ragaalayam Music Academy had no online presence, making it difficult for potential students in Bangalore to discover their courses or contact them.

**Solution:** A fully responsive, SEO-optimized website that:
- Showcases all 6 courses with structured details
- Provides a free trial class booking form for lead generation
- Displays contact information prominently for easy reach
- Ranks for local keywords like *"music classes Rajajinagar"* and *"carnatic music Bangalore"*

**Impact:** The website helps the academy reach new students online, establish credibility, and compete with larger music schools — all without any recurring platform cost.
